Mission Statement

The Carnatic Youth Society of Canada was conceived with the purpose of providing the youth in Canada an opportunity to prove themselves as talented Carnatic musicians. Canada is filled with many talented youth who are proficient in Carnatic music but are not given the opportunity to showcase their skills due to a myriad of factors. The CYSC endeavours to not only give a platform to perform, but opportunities to further hone their skills in workshops and provide opportunities to listen to veteran musicians of the field. Our vision for the future sees Canada as a hub for the art with homegrown artists who are performing at an elite level.

Why buy a membership?

Memberships will grant you entry to our events, as well as help us run future programs and provide even more opportunities for our talented youth to perform. This is an initiative for the youth, by the youth and we could use all of your support.

Types and Benefits

CYSC has membership packages. Memberships come in 2 varieties a Single Membership and Family Membership. By purchasing a membership, members will have a lot of unique benefits.

  • Emails about upcoming events
  • Access to our Monthly Concert Events
  • Access to Lecture Demonstrations

Sign up now using the form on the left or come visit us at our next event and ask one of our members about purchasing a memberships. Memberships can be picked up and paid for at any of our events.
